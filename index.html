Job Description

TechVerse Solutions is looking for an enthusiastic and eager-to-learn Junior Python Developer to join our team. This role is ideal for someone with basic programming skills who is passionate about building their career in software development. As a Junior Python Developer, you will be working alongside experienced developers on a variety of projects, gaining hands-on experience and building your technical skills in a professional setting.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist in writing basic Python code for various projects under the guidance of senior developers.
  • Help in testing and debugging code to ensure functionality and performance.
  • Contribute to the development of simple scripts and tools that support project needs.
  • Participate in team meetings and code reviews to learn best practices and improve your coding skills.
  • Collaborate with team members to complete tasks and meet project deadlines.
  • Learn and apply new technologies and programming techniques as needed.

Interview Process:

Our interview process includes a 30-minute session consisting of both HR and technical components:

  • HR Interview (10 minutes): Discuss your career goals, cultural fit, and how you align with our core principles.
  • Technical Interview (20 minutes): Review the project you selected in your application, and discuss your approach and learning experiences.

Application Tips:

  • Review Your Project: Understand your project well, and be prepared to discuss your approach and challenges.
  • Brush Up on Python Basics: Familiarize yourself with Python fundamentals, including data types, loops, and functions.
  • Polish Your CV and LinkedIn Profile: Ensure your CV is up-to-date and includes relevant skills, and make sure your LinkedIn profile is professional.
  • Prepare Questions: Come prepared with questions about the role, team, or company culture to demonstrate your interest.
